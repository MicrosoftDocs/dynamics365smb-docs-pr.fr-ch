## <a name="to-cancel-a-posted-sales-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="67b86-129">Pour annuler une facture vente validée</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="67b86-129">To cancel a posted sales invoice</span></span>
