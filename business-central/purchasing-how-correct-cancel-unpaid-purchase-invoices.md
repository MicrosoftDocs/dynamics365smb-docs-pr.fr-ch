## <a name="to-correct-a-posted-purchase-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="1f313-113">Pour corriger une facture achat validée</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-113">To correct a posted purchase invoice</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="1f313-115">Sélectionnez la facture achat validée à corriger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-115">Select the posted purchase invoice that you want to correct.</span></span>  

    > [!NOTE]  
    >   <span data-ttu-id="1f313-116">Si la case à cocher **Annulé** est activée, vous ne pouvez pas corriger la facture achat validée car elle l'a déjà été, ou a été annulée.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-116">If the **Canceled** check box is selected, then you cannot correct the posted purchase invoice because it has already been corrected or canceled.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="1f313-118">Une nouvelle facture achat avec les mêmes informations et dans laquelle vous pouvez apporter une correction est créée.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-118">A new purchase invoice with the same information is created where you can make the correction.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1f313-119">Pour plus d'informations, voir [Enregistrer des achats](purchasing-how-record-purchases.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-119">For more information, see [Record Purchases](purchasing-how-record-purchases.md).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1f313-120">La valeur du champ **Annulé** de la facture achat validée initiale devient **Oui**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-120">The **Canceled** field on the initial posted purchase invoice is changed to **Yes**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="1f313-121">Un avoir achat est automatiquement créé et validé pour annuler la facture achat validée initiale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f313-121">A purchase credit memo is automatically created and posted to void the initial posted purchase invoice.</span></span>
